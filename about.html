About Me

My Story

Born and raised in New York, I've always been fascinated by technology and how it can improve people's lives. My journey began when I got my first computer at the age of 10, sparking a lifelong passion for understanding how things work.

After completing my studies in Computer Science at NYU, I joined a small tech startup where I wore many hats and learned the ins and outs of software development and project management. This experience was invaluable and set me on the path to pursuing my MBA at Columbia University.

Throughout my career, I've been fortunate to work with talented teams and innovative companies that have shaped my professional outlook. I believe in the power of technology to solve complex problems and make meaningful impacts on society.

When I'm not working, you'll find me cycling through city parks, experimenting with new recipes, or planning my next travel adventure. I'm also actively involved in mentoring young professionals in the tech industry, as I believe in giving back to the community that has supported me.
